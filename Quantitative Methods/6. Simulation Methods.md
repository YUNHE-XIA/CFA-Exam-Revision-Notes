# 1. Normal and Lognormal Distributions | 正态分布与对数正态分布

## Definition | 定义

A positive random variable $Y$ is lognormally distributed if its natural logarithm, $\ln Y$, is normally distributed. Equivalently, if $X$ is normal and $Y=e^X$, then $Y$ is lognormal. Memory cue: **lognormal means “the log is normal.”**

若正随机变量 $Y$ 的自然对数 $\ln Y$ 服从正态分布，则 $Y$ 服从对数正态分布。等价地，若 $X$ 服从正态分布且 $Y=e^X$，则 $Y$ 服从对数正态分布。记忆方法：**对数正态即“取对数后为正态”。**

## Key Properties | 主要性质

| Distribution | Range | Shape | Typical Financial Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Normal | $(-\infty,+\infty)$ | Symmetric | Asset returns |
| Lognormal | $(0,+\infty)$ | Right-skewed; long right tail | Asset prices |

| 分布 | 取值范围 | 形状 | 常见金融用途 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 正态分布 | $(-\infty,+\infty)$ | 对称 | 资产收益率 |
| 对数正态分布 | $(0,+\infty)$ | 右偏；右尾较长 | 资产价格 |

Asset prices cannot fall below zero, so the lower bound of the lognormal distribution is economically appropriate. Returns can be negative and are often approximated by a normal distribution.

资产价格不能低于零，因此对数正态分布的下限具有经济意义。收益率可以为负，通常可用正态分布近似。

## Formula | 公式

Suppose $X$ is normal with mean $\mu$ and variance $\sigma^2$, and $Y=e^X$. Then:

假设 $X$ 服从均值为 $\mu$、方差为 $\sigma^2$ 的正态分布，且 $Y=e^X$，则：

$$
E(Y)=e^{\mu+\frac{1}{2}\sigma^2}
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(Y)=e^{2\mu+\sigma^2}(e^{\sigma^2}-1)
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $X$ | Associated normal random variable | 对应的正态随机变量 |
| $Y$ | Lognormal random variable | 对数正态随机变量 |
| $\mu$ | Mean of $X=\ln Y$ | $X=\ln Y$ 的均值 |
| $\sigma^2$ | Variance of $X=\ln Y$ | $X=\ln Y$ 的方差 |

## Interpretation | 解释

The lognormal variable's mean is not $e^\mu$. Because the distribution cannot extend below zero but can spread far to the right, higher $\sigma^2$ pushes its mean upward.

对数正态变量的均值并非 $e^\mu$。由于分布不能延伸至零以下，却可以向右侧大幅扩展，较高的 $\sigma^2$ 会提高其均值。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Do not confuse the parameters $\mu$ and $\sigma^2$ of $\ln Y$ with the mean and variance of $Y$.  
  不要把 $\ln Y$ 的参数 $\mu$、$\sigma^2$ 与 $Y$ 自身的均值、方差混淆。
- Normal returns can generate lognormal prices through exponentiation.  
  对正态收益率取指数，可得到对数正态价格。
- The lognormal distribution is bounded below by zero, not symmetric, and has no finite upper bound.  
  对数正态分布下限为零、非对称且无有限上限。

# 2. Continuously Compounded Returns and Asset Prices | 连续复利收益率与资产价格

## Definition | 定义

The continuously compounded return is the natural logarithm of the price relative. Multi-period continuously compounded return equals the sum of the subperiod continuously compounded returns.

连续复利收益率是价格比率的自然对数。多期连续复利收益率等于各子期连续复利收益率之和。

## Formula | 公式

$$
r_{0,T}=\ln\left(\frac{P_T}{P_0}\right)
$$

$$
P_T=P_0e^{r_{0,T}}
$$

$$
r_{0,T}=r_{0,1}+r_{1,2}+\cdots+r_{T-1,T}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $P_0$ | Current asset price | 当前资产价格 |
| $P_T$ | Asset price at time $T$ | $T$ 时点资产价格 |
| $r_{0,T}$ | Continuously compounded return from 0 to $T$ | 从 0 到 $T$ 的连续复利收益率 |

## IID Returns | 独立同分布收益率

If one-period continuously compounded returns are independently and identically distributed (i.i.d.) with mean $\mu$ and variance $\sigma^2$:

若单期连续复利收益率独立同分布（i.i.d.），均值为 $\mu$、方差为 $\sigma^2$：

$$
E(r_{0,T})=T\mu
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(r_{0,T})=T\sigma^2
$$

$$
\sigma(r_{0,T})=\sigma\sqrt{T}
$$

Independence means past returns do not predict future returns. Identical distribution implies stationarity: the return mean and variance do not change across periods.

独立性表示不能利用过去收益预测未来收益；同分布体现平稳性，即收益的均值与方差不随期间改变。

## Why Prices Are Modeled as Lognormal | 为何价格采用对数正态建模

If subperiod continuously compounded returns are normal, their sum $r_{0,T}$ is normal. Because $P_T=P_0e^{r_{0,T}}$, future price $P_T$ is lognormal. Even if subperiod returns are not normal, their sum may be approximately normal under the central limit theorem, supporting an approximate lognormal price model.

若各子期连续复利收益率服从正态分布，其和 $r_{0,T}$ 也服从正态分布。由于 $P_T=P_0e^{r_{0,T}}$，未来价格 $P_T$ 服从对数正态分布。即使单期收益并非正态，根据中心极限定理，其和也可能近似正态，因此价格仍可近似采用对数正态模型。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Returns add across time; prices compound through exponentiation.  
  收益率跨期相加；价格通过指数形式复利增长。
- Mean scales with $T$, variance scales with $T$, and standard deviation scales with $\sqrt{T}$ under independence.  
  在独立假设下，均值按 $T$ 放大，方差按 $T$ 放大，标准差按 $\sqrt{T}$ 放大。
- Normality is preserved by a linear combination of normal variables.  
  正态变量的线性组合仍服从正态分布。

# 3. Volatility and Annualization | 波动率与年化

## Definition | 定义

Volatility is the standard deviation of continuously compounded returns on the underlying asset and is conventionally reported on an annualized basis.

波动率是标的资产连续复利收益率的标准差，通常以年化形式报告。

## Formula | 公式

For $N$ independent trading periods per year:

若一年含 $N$ 个相互独立的交易期：

$$
\sigma_{annual}=\sigma_{period}\sqrt{N}
$$

$$
\mu_{annual}=N\mu_{period}
$$

For daily data, the curriculum uses approximately $N=250$ trading days.

对于日度数据，本课程使用每年约 $N=250$ 个交易日。

## Worked Example: Astra International | 计算示例：Astra International

Given | 已知：

| Day | Closing Price (IDR) |
|---|---:|
| Monday | 6,950 |
| Tuesday | 7,000 |
| Wednesday | 6,850 |
| Thursday | 6,600 |
| Friday | 6,350 |

Calculation | 计算：

$$
r_t=\ln\left(\frac{P_t}{P_{t-1}}\right)
$$

| Period | Continuously Compounded Return |
|---|---:|
| Mon–Tue | 0.7168% |
| Tue–Wed | -2.1661% |
| Wed–Thu | -3.7179% |
| Thu–Fri | -3.8615% |

The sample daily standard deviation is approximately $2.1261\%$.

日收益率的样本标准差约为 $2.1261\%$。

$$
\sigma_{annual}=0.0212608\sqrt{250}=33.62\%
$$

The sample mean daily return is approximately $-2.2572\%$.

日收益率样本均值约为 $-2.2572\%$。

$$
\mu_{annual}=250(-2.2572\%)=-564.29\%
$$

Answer | 答案：

- Estimated annualized volatility: **33.62%**.  
  估计年化波动率：**33.62%**。
- Mechanically annualized expected continuously compounded return: **−564.29%**. This estimate is not prudent because only four daily observations from an unusually turbulent week are used.  
  机械年化的预期连续复利收益率：**−564.29%**。该估计并不可靠，因为样本仅含异常动荡一周中的四个日收益率。
- If daily continuously compounded returns are normal, share prices are lognormal.  
  若日连续复利收益率服从正态分布，则股价服从对数正态分布。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Annualize variance by multiplying by $N$; annualize standard deviation by multiplying by $\sqrt{N}$.  
  方差年化乘以 $N$；标准差年化乘以 $\sqrt{N}$。
- A statistically correct calculation can still be economically unreliable when the sample is small or unrepresentative.  
  即使计算在统计上正确，小样本或缺乏代表性的样本仍可能使结果在经济上不可靠。

# 4. Monte Carlo Simulation | 蒙特卡洛模拟

## Definition | 定义

Monte Carlo simulation generates many random samples from one or more specified probability distributions. Repeated simulation trials create a distribution of possible outcomes from which values, risks, and probabilities can be estimated.

蒙特卡洛模拟从一个或多个指定概率分布中大量随机抽样。重复模拟试验形成可能结果的分布，据此估计价值、风险和概率。

## Investment Applications | 投资应用

- Estimate portfolio profit and loss, return, and risk over a specified horizon.  
  估计特定期间内投资组合的损益、收益与风险。
- Value complex securities and contingent claims when no analytical pricing formula is available.  
  当不存在解析定价公式时，对复杂证券和或有索赔进行估值。
- Model securities with complex embedded options, such as mortgage-backed securities.  
  建模含复杂嵌入期权的证券，例如抵押贷款支持证券。
- Test model sensitivity to changes in key assumptions.  
  测试模型对关键假设变化的敏感性。
- Incorporate non-normality, fat tails, tail dependence, and multiple risk factors through assumed distributions.  
  通过设定分布纳入非正态、肥尾、尾部相关和多个风险因子。

## Six-Step Process | 六步流程

1. **Specify the quantity of interest and underlying variables.** Set their starting values.  
   **确定目标量和基础变量。** 设定基础变量的初始值。
2. **Specify the time grid.** Divide the horizon into $K$ subperiods, with time increment $\Delta t$.  
   **设定时间网格。** 将期限划分为 $K$ 个子期间，每期长度为 $\Delta t$。
3. **Specify the data-generating model.** Choose distributions and parameters for key risk factors.  
   **设定数据生成模型。** 为关键风险因子选择分布及参数。
4. **Generate simulated paths.** Draw random risk-factor values and convert them into paths for the underlying variables.  
   **生成模拟路径。** 抽取风险因子随机值，并转换为基础变量的路径。
5. **Calculate the outcome and present value.** This completes one simulation trial.  
   **计算结果及现值。** 至此完成一次模拟试验。
6. **Repeat for $I$ trials and summarize.** Use the simulated distribution to estimate the quantity of interest.  
   **重复 $I$ 次并汇总。** 使用模拟结果分布估计目标量。

Steps 1–3 specify the simulation; Steps 4–6 run and summarize it.

步骤 1–3 用于设定模拟；步骤 4–6 用于运行和汇总模拟。

## Stock Price Process | 股价过程

One possible simulated price-change model is:

一种可能的模拟价格变动模型为：

$$
\Delta S=\mu S\Delta t+\sigma S\sqrt{\Delta t}Z_k
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $S$ | Prior stock price | 上一期股价 |
| $\Delta S$ | Simulated stock price change | 模拟股价变动 |
| $\mu$ | Assumed mean parameter | 假设的均值参数 |
| $\sigma$ | Assumed standard deviation parameter | 假设的标准差参数 |
| $\Delta t$ | Length of one time step | 单个时间步长 |
| $Z_k$ | Standard normal draw for subperiod $k$ | 子期间 $k$ 的标准正态随机数 |

## Contingent Claim Example | 或有索赔示例

For an Asian-style claim whose payoff is the positive difference between the final price and the average price:

对于到期支付“最终价格高于期间平均价格的正差额”的亚式或有索赔：

$$
C_{i,T}=\max(S_{i,T}-\bar{S}_i,0)
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $C_{i,T}$ | Claim payoff at maturity in trial $i$ | 第 $i$ 次试验的到期支付 |
| $S_{i,T}$ | Final stock price in trial $i$ | 第 $i$ 次试验的最终股价 |
| $\bar{S}_i$ | Average stock price over the claim's life in trial $i$ | 第 $i$ 次试验中存续期平均股价 |

The estimated claim value is the average discounted payoff across all trials. In the source simulation, 654 of 1,000 trials produced zero payoff, so the estimated probability of zero payoff was $65.4\%$.

估计价值等于全部试验贴现支付的平均值。在材料的模拟中，1,000 次试验有 654 次支付为零，因此零支付概率估计为 $65.4\%$。

For a lookback claim based on the minimum price before maturity:

对于基于到期前最低价格的回望型或有索赔：

$$
C_{i,T}=\max(S_{i,T}-S_{i,min},0)
$$

## Strengths and Limitations | 优点与局限

| Strengths | Limitations |
|---|---|
| Flexible for complex, path-dependent securities | Produces statistical estimates, not exact values |
| Permits sensitivity and “what-if” analysis | Results depend on model and distribution assumptions |
| Handles multiple risk factors and non-normal distributions | Provides less direct cause-and-effect insight than analytical methods |

| 优点 | 局限 |
|---|---|
| 可处理复杂及路径依赖证券 | 只能得到统计估计，并非精确值 |
| 可进行敏感性与“如果……会怎样”分析 | 结果依赖模型及分布假设 |
| 可处理多个风险因子和非正态分布 | 相比解析方法，对因果关系的揭示较弱 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Monte Carlo simulation draws from an **assumed probability distribution**; it does not require resampling historical observations.  
  蒙特卡洛模拟从**假设的概率分布**中抽样，并不要求对历史观测值重抽样。
- More trials reduce simulation noise but do not correct a misspecified model.  
  增加试验次数可降低模拟噪声，但无法纠正错误的模型设定。
- Monte Carlo complements analytical methods; it does not independently establish cause-and-effect relationships.  
  蒙特卡洛模拟是解析方法的补充，不能独立建立因果关系。

# 5. Bootstrap Resampling | 自助法重抽样

## Definition | 定义

Bootstrap is a resampling method that treats the observed sample as if it were the population. It repeatedly draws samples **with replacement**, normally using the same sample size as the original, to approximate a sampling distribution.

自助法把观测样本视为总体，反复进行**有放回抽样**，且每个重抽样样本通常与原样本等大，从而近似构建抽样分布。

Because sampling is with replacement, an observation may appear multiple times in one bootstrap sample, while another observation may not appear at all.

由于采用有放回抽样，同一观测值可能在某个重抽样样本中出现多次，而其他观测值可能完全没有出现。

## Uses | 用途

- Estimate standard errors.  
  估计标准误。
- Construct confidence intervals for population parameters.  
  构建总体参数的置信区间。
- Infer means, variances, skewness, kurtosis, or other statistics without relying on a specific analytical formula.  
  无需依赖特定解析公式，推断均值、方差、偏度、峰度或其他统计量。
- Simulate investment values using an empirical distribution of observed price changes or returns.  
  使用观测价格变动或收益率形成的经验分布模拟投资价值。

## Bootstrap Simulation Process | 自助法模拟流程

1. Specify the quantity of interest, underlying variables, and starting values.  
   确定目标量、基础变量及初始值。
2. Choose a time grid consistent with the frequency of the observations.  
   选择与观测数据频率一致的时间网格。
3. Use observed historical price changes or returns as the empirical distribution.  
   将历史价格变动或收益率作为经验分布。
4. Draw $K$ observations with replacement and convert them into a simulated price path.  
   有放回地抽取 $K$ 个观测值，并转换为模拟价格路径。
5. Calculate the claim payoff and discount it to the present.  
   计算或有索赔支付并贴现至当前。
6. Repeat for $I$ trials and average or summarize the results.  
   重复 $I$ 次，对结果求平均或进行汇总。

The main operational difference from Monte Carlo simulation is Step 3: bootstrap draws from the empirical sample rather than a specified theoretical distribution.

与蒙特卡洛模拟相比，主要操作差异在第 3 步：自助法从经验样本抽样，而非从指定的理论分布抽样。

## Strengths and Limitations | 优点与局限

| Strengths | Limitations |
|---|---|
| Makes fewer parametric distribution assumptions | Depends on the observed sample being representative |
| Useful when analytical standard-error or confidence-interval formulas are difficult | Individual resampled draws are limited to values observed in the empirical sample |
| Preserves empirical distribution features present in the data | Produces statistical estimates, not exact results |

| 优点 | 局限 |
|---|---|
| 较少依赖参数化分布假设 | 依赖观测样本具有代表性 |
| 当标准误或置信区间的解析公式难以获得时很有用 | 单次重抽样只能抽到经验样本中已经出现的数值 |
| 保留数据中已有的经验分布特征 | 只能得到统计估计，并非精确结果 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **With replacement** is essential; without replacement, it is not standard bootstrap resampling.  
  **有放回抽样**是关键；无放回抽样不是标准自助法。
- Each bootstrap resample is normally the same size as the original sample.  
  每个自助法重抽样样本通常与原样本等大。
- Bootstrap quality cannot exceed the information contained in the observed sample.  
  自助法结果的质量受限于观测样本所包含的信息。

# 6. Monte Carlo vs. Bootstrap | 蒙特卡洛模拟与自助法对比

| Feature | Monte Carlo Simulation | Bootstrap Resampling |
|---|---|---|
| Source of random draws | Assumed theoretical distribution | Observed empirical sample |
| Sampling method | Draw from specified model | Draw with replacement |
| Main input risk | Model and parameter misspecification | Unrepresentative historical sample |
| Main advantage | Flexible modeling of complex and hypothetical outcomes | Fewer parametric assumptions |
| Common output | Distribution of values, returns, losses, or payoffs | Sampling distribution, standard error, confidence interval, or empirical valuation |

| 特征 | 蒙特卡洛模拟 | 自助法重抽样 |
|---|---|---|
| 随机抽样来源 | 假设的理论分布 | 观测到的经验样本 |
| 抽样方式 | 从指定模型抽样 | 有放回抽样 |
| 主要输入风险 | 模型或参数设定错误 | 历史样本缺乏代表性 |
| 主要优点 | 可灵活建模复杂及假设性结果 | 较少依赖参数化假设 |
| 常见输出 | 价值、收益、损失或支付的分布 | 抽样分布、标准误、置信区间或经验估值 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Both methods use repetitive sampling and produce statistical estimates. The fastest distinction is the source of random draws: **distribution/model = Monte Carlo; observed sample = bootstrap**.

两种方法都依赖重复抽样并产生统计估计。最快区分方法是看随机抽样来源：**理论分布/模型 = 蒙特卡洛；观测样本 = 自助法**。

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Key Formula | 核心公式

$$
P_T=P_0e^{r_{0,T}}
$$

Normal continuously compounded return $\Rightarrow$ Lognormal future price.

连续复利收益率服从正态分布 $\Rightarrow$ 未来价格服从对数正态分布。

## 2. Time Scaling | 时间尺度转换

$$
E(r_{0,T})=T\mu
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(r_{0,T})=T\sigma^2
$$

$$
\sigma(r_{0,T})=\sigma\sqrt{T}
$$

Mean and variance scale with $T$; standard deviation scales with $\sqrt{T}$.

均值与方差按 $T$ 放大；标准差按 $\sqrt{T}$ 放大。

## 3. Key Relationship | 核心关系

Monte Carlo draws from an assumed distribution; bootstrap draws with replacement from observed data.

蒙特卡洛从假设分布抽样；自助法从观测数据中有放回抽样。

## 4. Common Mistake | 常见错误

Do not assume that more simulation trials eliminate model risk. More trials reduce random simulation error only.

不要认为增加模拟次数可以消除模型风险；增加次数只能降低随机模拟误差。

## 5. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

- **“Log is normal”** $\Rightarrow$ the variable is lognormal.  
  **“对数为正态”** $\Rightarrow$ 原变量为对数正态。
- **Specified distribution** $\Rightarrow$ Monte Carlo.  
  **指定分布** $\Rightarrow$ 蒙特卡洛。
- **Observed sample + replacement** $\Rightarrow$ Bootstrap.  
  **观测样本 + 有放回** $\Rightarrow$ 自助法。
